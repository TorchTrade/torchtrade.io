Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

1. What We Collect

The TorchTrade website (torchtrade.io) is a static website. We do not collect personal data directly. When you purchase a product, payment processing is handled by Paddle (paddle.com), who acts as the Merchant of Record. Paddle collects the information necessary to process your payment (name, email, payment details) under their own privacy policy.

2. Cookies

This website uses only a single local storage entry to remember your theme preference (dark/light mode). We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party cookies.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Paddle — payment processing and product delivery. See Paddle's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • GitHub API — fetching repository star/fork counts (no personal data involved).

4. Data We Receive from Paddle

After a purchase, Paddle may share your email address with us for the purpose of delivering your digital product and providing support. We do not use this email for marketing unless you explicitly opt in.

5. Data Retention

We retain purchase records (email, product purchased, date) for the purpose of providing product updates and support. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. For data held by Paddle, contact Paddle directly. For any data we hold, contact us via GitHub.

7. Changes

We may update this policy as needed. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us via GitHub at github.com/TorchTrade.
